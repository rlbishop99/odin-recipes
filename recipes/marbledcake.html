Marbled Cake

Description

This is regular freaking cake but with marbles in it. Ever heard of russian roulette?

Ingredients

  • One box of Betty Crocker Crap
  • Marbles. LOTS of marbles.
  • Paintball Gun? (x1)

Steps

  1. Bake the vanilla cake with lots of love, using the instructions on the box.
  2. Ignore the sounds of marbles being loaded into the paintball gun.
  3. Burst into tears as your son demolishes the cake with marbles he's shooting from the paintball gun.
  4. Try and enjoy the remaining scraps of the cake you worked so hard to bake, as your delinquent son giggles to himself in the background.
